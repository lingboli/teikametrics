case studies

Aubuchon Hardware

Problem

Aubuchon Hardware is the oldest family owned hardware retail in the United States. They carry many products that smaller retailers don't have access to. However, they weren't able to leverage their extensive inventory advantage. Prices were assigned without dynamic pricing technology, so many products were mispriced or underpriced, causing them to take a loss on each transaction. With 30,000+ products, they couldn't sift through them one by one to figure out the optimal price.

Solution

We defined a new pricing strategy using predictive analytics that took advantage of Aubuchon's extensive inventory.


With the Teikametrics software platform, their pricing now adjusts in real-time for market conditions and competitors' prices to maximize Aubuchon's profits.

Results

  • 125% revenue increase
  • Increase in Gross Margin
  • 5% increase in Average Order Value
