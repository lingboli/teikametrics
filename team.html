Data is our passion

We're bringing data science to online retail

our mission

We don't want to make just another suite of enterprise software. We want to change the way the industry works.

After working for years as ecommerce retailers, we saw the promise in a new way of running a business: one based on analytics. One that took advantage of data mining and automation, so we could focus on bigger questions. One that could help even the largest, most complex customers grow.

the team

Our Leadership

Alasdair McLean Foreman

CEO

As the Chief Executive Officer, Alasdair is responsible for Teikametrics’ overall business strategy and day-to-day operations. Alasdair founded an e-commerce company in his dorm-room which grew into a multi-million dollar company selling high-end sporting goods. He also founded the weight loss and fitness company Traineo and has built and provided e-commerce solutions to large organizations including Newscorp, The Times of London, L’Oreal, and The New York Marathon.

Alasdair earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics at Harvard University where he was captain of the Track and Field Team and a member of the Great Britain and England national track teams.

Joe Gaetano

CTO

As the Chief Technology Officer, Joseph drives the company’s innovation and technology development. Joseph was the CTO of Traineo, a leading weight loss and fitness website, and previously founded a technology company providing web-based management solutions to small businesses. Joseph’s B2B experience includes advanced network and infrastructure management for several Fortune 500 companies.

Roberta Foster

Sales & Marketing Director

Before joining Teikametrics, Roberta was a sales and marketing coordinator at 89 Degrees, a marketing agency specializing in customer engagement for consumer retail brands including IKEA and Hyundai. At Teikametrics, she oversees the entire sales team and is responsible for generating new sales opportunities and revenue. 

Earlier, Roberta served in a marketing management role at one of the leading beauty spas in New England. She holds a BA in Marketing from the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth.

Tony Vec

Lead Development Engineer

Tony discovered his love for programming in his junior year of high school. Since he has developed a broad knowledge of programming languages and information systems ranging from Ruby, Java, Android, Python, and Ubuntu. He currently leads the development of our data analytics platform using the latest database technologies.

Prior to joining Teikametrics, Tony worked as an undergraduate researcher at the Center for Intelligent Information Retrieval at UMass Amherst. He holds a Computer Science degree from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

Shijun Wu

Senior Development Engineer

Shijun has rapidly developed his coding prowess since he started tinkering early in high school. His strong mathematical background has enabled him to effectively build solutions to complex problems involving large datasets. At Teikametrics, Shijun is at the forefront of new product development and research.

Earlier, Shijun worked as a researcher for the Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) at UMass Amherst where he researched and developed computational models to improve online deliberations. He holds a Computer Science degree from the University of Massachusetts Amherst where he received Dean's List honors for every semester.

Tyler Goodspeed

Advisor

As a strategic advisor to the Teikametrics team, Tyler guides the development of mathematical analysis into pricing strategy, competitive game dynamics, and advanced modeling to enhance the company’s competitive edge. This analysis has helped Teikametrics to develop proprietary competitive strategies and pricing models that are a unique component of our services.

Tyler is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Economics at Harvard University. At Harvard, he also earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and History with highest honors, following a year at the University of Cambridge as a Gates Scholar.
