our (not so) secret ingredient

We do it for you, so you don't have to.

Data is a powerful, but it's meaningless unless you know how to use it. Configuration is tricky and time consuming. Mistakes can cost you thousands. That's where we come in - we'll examine your pain points, create a strategy, and customize settings using our deep ecommerce experience.

1) Strategy

First, we need to know what kind of retailer you are. Do you sell high-end low volume, collectible, or used inventory? Seasonal? High volume, low margin? We'll chat about your pain points and walk you through a systematic review of your business to create a Client Profile.

2) Setup

We draw on our deep ecommerce experience to create a winning strategy for you. Then, we roll up our sleeves and handle the implementation: setting up APIs, configuring custom rules, and automating import of new products. It's completely customizable.

3) Support

In addition to ongoing training, you'll get a dedicated account manager who will handle any questions you have. Markets change rapidly, so we're constantly updating and improving the software to keep you one step ahead of your competitors.
